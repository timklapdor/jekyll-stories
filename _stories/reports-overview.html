This is the first series of reports cards on NSW trout fishery monitoring streams. These streams where selected with stakeholders at the trout strategy workshops in 2018. These streams are designed to be indicators on how the fishery is tracking across the 3 regions and across the state and are less a report card on each river or to provide specific information on what fish are in each site.

How to use these report cards

Use these report cards in conjunction with the NSW stocking website (link) to be informed on how the fishery is tracking in your area or an area you plan the fish. The stocking information is updated regularly and has all NSW stocking sites shown with in it.

As a combination it is hoped they will give you a good chance on landing some fish on your next trout fishing adventure!
